1. General provisions

This Personal Data Processing Policy (hereinafter referred to as the Policy) has been developed in accordance with the Law of the Republic of Kazakhstan "On Personal Data and their Protection" and other regulatory acts of the Republic of Kazakhstan. The Policy defines the procedure for processing and protecting personal data of individuals transferred to Yeta LLP (hereinafter referred to as the Operator) through the website, feedback forms, as well as within the framework of employment, civil law and other contractual relations.


2. Terms

  • Personal data is information related to a directly or indirectly identifiable natural person.
  • Personal data processing — any actions with personal data: collection, storage, modification, use, transfer, blocking, depersonalization and destruction.
  • The operator is Yeta LLP, which independently determines the purposes and procedure for processing personal data.
  • Personal data subject is an individual whose personal data is being processed.


3. Legal grounds and purposes of processing

Personal data is processed by the Operator on the following grounds:

  • consent of the personal data subject;
  • fulfillment of obligations under labor, civil law and other contracts;
  • compliance with the requirements of the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.

Processing purposes:

  • conclusion and execution of contracts;
  • personnel accounting;
  • providing access to online services and feedback;
  • provision of marketing and informational newsletters (subject to consent);
  • obtaining anonymized analytics.


4. Categories of personal data

  • Full name, contact information, IIN, residence/registration address;
  • information about employment, education, qualifications;
  • data provided by the users of the site through the forms;
  • other information necessary to achieve the specified goals.

5. Processing and storage conditions

Personal data is processed in compliance with the principles of legality, fairness and confidentiality, to the extent necessary to achieve specific, predetermined goals.

The shelf life is no longer than required for processing purposes. Upon achievement of the goals, the data is destroyed or depersonalized, except in cases provided for by the legislation of the Republic of Kazakhstan.


6. Transfer to third parties

The operator can transfer personal data:

  • government agencies within the framework of the law;
  • to banks (for settlements under the agreement);
  • contractors and partners (subject to consent);
  • to other persons — solely on the basis of the written consent of the subject or the requirements stipulated by law.


7. Rights of the personal data subject

The subject has the right to:

  • to receive information about the processing of their data;
  • to withdraw consent;
  • to request that your personal data be changed, blocked, or deleted;
  • to defend their rights in court and in the authorized body for personal data protection (Ministry of Digital Development, Innovation and Aerospace Industry of the Republic of Kazakhstan).


8. Measures to protect personal data

The Operator takes all necessary legal, organizational and technical measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, modification, disclosure, destruction:

  • access restriction;
  • using secure communication channels;-
  • antivirus protection and encryption;
  • appointment of a responsible person;
  • staff training.


9. Policy Changes

The Operator has the right to make changes to this Policy. The current version is posted on the website and takes effect from the moment of publication.
