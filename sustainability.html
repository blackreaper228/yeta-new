That’s why our ESG strategy includes real action in three areas:

Environment

Reducing Environmental Impact through Route Optimization, Energy-Efficient Transport, and Digitalization

We reduce our environmental impact through route optimization, energy-efficient transport, and digitalization of processes to minimize paperwork and waste.

Social

Supporting Education and Youth Innovation in Engineering through Partnerships and International Projects

We actively support education and youth in engineering.

  • Official memorandum with KBTU to promote technical education.

  • Support of student teams in the Shell Eco Marathon, helping future engineers test their innovations on the international stage.

  • Engagement with local students and universities to raise awareness of logistics and energy efficiency careers.

We believe sustainable growth starts from within — with our people.

Governance

Fostering Sustainable Growth through Employee Development, Professional Training, and Well-Being Initiatives

  • Every employee completes an individual development plan with a certified coach at the beginning of each year.

  • We hold strategic team sessions twice a year to set clear goals and strengthen collaboration.

  • Regular professional training and industry certifications.

  • Transparent motivation and rewards system.

  • Focus on mental well-being and comfortable working conditions.
